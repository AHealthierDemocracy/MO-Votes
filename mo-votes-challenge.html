ℹ️ About Vot-ER

Vot-ER is bridging a gap in care with a groundbreaking prescription: civic engagement. Our mission is to empower patients and healthcare professionals to address the root causes of health disparities through civic participation. Our swift and easy HIPAA-compliant resources promote employee happiness, combat burnout, and cultivate deeper trust between healthcare professionals and patients.

Since 2020, we've expanded our nonpartisan work to 900+ participating sites with 50,000+ health advocates and professionals. Featured by The New York Times, CNN, Washington Post, NBC, NPR, and PBS NewsHour. Recognizing that 83% of adults visit a healthcare facility each year, we understand the critical role these spaces play in voter registration and turnout — which directly impact public health and investment.
