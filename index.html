About MO Votes

Healthcare has a role to play in civic health

As healthcare professionals in Missouri, we know the health of our communities is tied to a strong democracy. MO Votes is a nonpartisan movement that integrates voter registration and civic participation into healthcare settings — because when our neighbors have a voice in democracy, they have a voice in their community's well-being.

We work with hospitals, FQHCs, clinics, associations, and advocacy organizations across Missouri to make civic engagement a natural part of care. Our tools are HIPAA-compliant, easy to deploy, and built for busy healthcare environments.

Whether you're an individual provider or a large health system, there's a place for you in this work.

Get Involved

Three ways to participate

No experience necessary — just a desire to build a healthy democracy across Missouri communities.

  1. Step 01
    Integrate voter registration into your practice

    An easy first step to spark a healthy democracy. Our HIPAA-compliant tools do the heavy lifting so you can help patients and colleagues get registered with a few simple steps.

  2. Step 02
    Recruit Missouri healthcare professionals

    Help build the community by sharing this campaign with your peers and colleagues. Your network is one of the most powerful tools in this work.

  3. Step 03
    Join the planning committee

    Ready for a leadership role? Help design and implement civic engagement campaigns and advocate for your community's health on a larger scale.
